Money Heist


      Money Heist (Spanish: La casa de papel, "The House of Paper") is a Spanish heist crime drama television series created
by Álex Pina, follows the Professor (Álvaro Morte), a criminal mastermind who brings eight thieves together to take
hostages and lock themselves in the Royal Mint of Spain as he manipulates the police to carry out his plan.

AUDIENCE REVIEWS



If you are a Hollywood super-hero movie fan, then you will not like this, the story line reflects a real life situations, ok some of the characters are dumb and behave erratically, but that also happens in the real world. For the people who rate this show poorly all i can say is, go watch spider-man or other movies alike. Can't wait for season 5.



Have to admit that after reading the description of this show, I didn't expect myself to get so immersed and feel so emotional while watching it. I though it would be one more of those simple yet fun heist movies without much more involved than the heist itself. However, while watching it you get to understand that this show is more than just a money heist. It becomes an eye-opener about the way our world is run by capitalism and economics. It certainly has a deeper meaning and the makers of this show have found a great way of delivering it to us. There is not much to say about it. Even the actors where great and their acting was so believable that makes me wonder why many of them don't have a career in Hollywood... This show is smart, it delivers great messages, it has great tempo leaving you no time to get bored or anything and you will absolutely feel thankful for watching it!
